About Click

We don't just take photos.
We make memories.

Click is the official Film & Photography Society of Shri Ram College of Commerce, Delhi University — a community for visual storytellers, frame-chasers, and cinephiles.

From street photography and studio portraits to short film production and documentary screenings, we explore every dimension of the visual medium together.

What We Do

Annual Event

Impressions

Our flagship annual event — a celebration of visual art bringing together photographers, filmmakers, and storytellers from across Delhi University.

Annual · Flagship
02
Monthly

Film Screening

Curated screenings of world cinema — classics, indie films, documentaries, and student productions — followed by open discussions.

Monthly
03
Workshop

Photography Masterclass

Hands-on sessions with professionals covering composition, lighting, storytelling, and post-processing — for all skill levels.

Every Semester
04
Outing

Photo Walks

Guided photography outings through Old Delhi, Lodhi Art District, and hidden corners of the city — capture the streets with your community.

Monthly
05
Production

Short Filmmaking

An intense filmmaking sprint where teams conceive, shoot, and edit a complete short film — from script to screen in under 48 hours.

Every Semester

Event Coverage

Official photography and videography coverage for society events, fests, and campus life — documenting SRCC's most memorable moments.
